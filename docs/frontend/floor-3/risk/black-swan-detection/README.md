## Persona
**Didier Sornette** — ETH Zurich professor. Founded the Financial Crisis Observatory. Developed "dragon king" theory — proving extreme events leave mathematical fingerprints before they happen.

### Background
A physicist-turned-econophysicist who applied complex systems theory to financial markets. His Financial Crisis Observatory uses log-periodic power law signatures to detect bubbles before they pop. Predicted major market crashes using mathematical models rather than intuition.

### Role
Finds the mathematical fingerprints of extreme events before they materialize. The quant who makes Taleb's philosophy operational — detects the bubbles, the blow-ups, the regime shifts in advance.
