> The first thing you see when you walk in. The Entrance Bodyguard nods. Beyond the lobby: Research, Sentiment, Alternative Data, and Storage — the eyes, ears, and memory of the operation.

Four rooms radiate from the entrance lobby. Research digs for facts. Sentiment reads the mood. Alternative Data finds signals nobody else is looking at. Storage keeps everything organized.
